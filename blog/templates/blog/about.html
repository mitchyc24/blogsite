About

Hi, my name is Mitchell Carroll and this is my personal about page.

Background

I grew up in a rural part of Quebec, Canada. I've always been interested in science and technology which led me tu pursue a Computer Science degree from the Royal Military College of Canada. I served 13 years in the Canadian Air Force as an Aerospace Engineer, with postings int Nova Scotia and Ontario. I have had short deployments to the north including Iqaluit, Iceland, and Scotland. I worked as a maintenance line officer, quality assurance manager, and as a electronic warfare support officer. I have since retired from the forces to pursue my interests elsewhere.

Interests

I am interested in a wide range of topics, including science, engineering, and physics. I enjoy solving problems and finding new ways to approach challenges. In my spare time, I enjoy playing chess and engaging with my pets and the natural world. I have a curiosity about animals and enjoy exploring geography and the vastness of space. I am also skilled in programming and use my knowledge to create new solutions. In addition to these technical pursuits, I also value physical and mental well-being and engage in activities like yoga, calisthenics, and mobility exercises. My interests also include futurism and humanism, and I am interested in considering the long-term impacts of technological and societal developments on the human experience.
